Our history

Founded in 2004, QualityMad is an agro-industry company that began with fresh lychee fruit and has expanded towards vanilla pods, cloves, cinnamon, pink peppercorn and others local spices.
Quality and Proactiveness as our strongest values, we go on keeping high quality in our products and services, international standards of food safety, ethics, sustainable agriculture and long-term relationship with farmers, employees and customers.

At Your Service

Driving Growth & Empowering Communities

With a large production capacity and seasonal employment for over 400 people, we are proud to support economic development while delivering excellence at scale.

Built for Efficiency, Positioned for Reach

Our 600 m² state-of-the-art facility, strategically located just 10 minutes from the seaport, enables seamless logistics and is primed for future expansion.

Collaborating for a Sustainable Future

From managing a growers' association to developing new markets and sulfur-free treatments, we work hand-in-hand with collectors and partners to elevate quality and sustainability.

Tailored Solutions, Trusted Standards

We offer flexible, high-quality packaging under private and MadPremium labels and ensure global shipping by air or sea—all backed by strict certifications including GlobalGap, Organic, and Fairtrade.
